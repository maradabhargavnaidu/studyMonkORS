Key Features

Automated Job Posting

Automated job posting is the use of technology to automatically distribute job advertisements across multiple online platforms, saving time and increasing the reach of job listings to attract a larger pool of potential candidates. It streamlines the process, ensuring consistency and providing tracking and analytics for effective recruitment strategies.

Smart Applicant Tracking

Smart applicant tracking refers to the use of artificial intelligence and advanced algorithms to automate and streamline the recruitment process, from receiving and sorting applications to identifying top candidates. It improves efficiency by reducing manual effort and provides insights for better decision-making in hiring.
